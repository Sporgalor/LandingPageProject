About me!

Hey all! I'm Crunchy! I'm 26 years old, I have three sweet cats! I am a variety streamer, but we usually rotate the same 2-3 games per week. Once we finish one, we move another in! You will likely see cozy games, metroidvania's, platformer's, indie games and the like! I will try just about any game at least once! My favorite color is yellow and purple, mint chocolate chip ice cream is the best flavor, and I think Otters/cats are the cutest! Let me know your favorites in chat! <3
